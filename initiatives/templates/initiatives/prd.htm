PUBLIC RELATIONS DEPARTMENT

The Public Relations Department or PRD, is the face of Nirmaan Organisation, Pilani chapter. PRD is divided into subsections which are content writing, videography, graphic designing, app, and website development. We work closely with the volunteers and alumni associated with Nirmaan and help create awareness about their work both inside and outside of campus. We also undertake the publicity for events like Nirmaanâ€™s flagship donation drive: SORAD(Save One Rupee A Day), GREEN week which is celebrated prior to the tech-fest APOGEE, Oasis(Cultural Fest) stalls selling products manufactured by our beneficiaries, creating invites for gen-Bitsians for several events and seminars like Joy Of Giving Week among many others. We also help to collaborate with other NGOs.The medium for that is through posters, WhatsApp posts, videos, and our Instagram page, through the continuous hard work of the members of PRD we are trying to reach as many people as possible. Our main motive is to connect people who want to help but can't due to time constraints or the knowledge of this domain and people who need help, through Nirmaan volunteers. We are trying to maximize our reach to help people for the same cause.
